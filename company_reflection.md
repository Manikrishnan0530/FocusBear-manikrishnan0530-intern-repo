## Why do you think Focus Bear was created?
Focus Bear was created to help people, especially those with ADHD or similar challenges, improve their daily routines and build healthy habits consistently.

## What problem is Focus Bear solving?
Focus Bear helps people reduce distractions while working and stay consistent with their daily routines. It supports users in maintaining focus and not skipping important tasks or habits.

## Why do you think this mission is important?
This mission is important because staying focused and completing tasks on time is a common challenge. By reducing distractions and improving focus, it helps people become more productive and maintain a better work environment.
